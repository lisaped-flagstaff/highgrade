Welcome to Highgrade Personnel

HIGHGRADE PERSONNEL PTY LTD is a privately owned Mining Recruitment Company established in 2017 in Kalgoorlie, Western Australia. With offices in Como and Perth, we provide dedicated support to city-based clients and our specialized FIFO workforce.

In 2020, we expanded our reach with the Technical Professional Recruitment Division. We combine local expertise with a global mindset to deliver outcomes in:

  • Mining & Civil Engineering
  • Rail & Road Infrastructure
  • Renewables Infrastructure
  • Construction Operations
  • Light Rail & Metro
  • Technical Niches

At HIGHGRADE, we pride ourselves on flexibility and a relentless drive to exceed client expectations in the ever-evolving Western Australian market.
